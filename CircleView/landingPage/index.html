    Microsoft Teams Didn’t Solve My Problems

    Despite being a useful general tool, Microsoft Teams has problems. Teams is meant for a wide variety of users, and requires extensive customization for manufacturing use. The decentralized nature of Teams leads to many team rooms; thus users can have dozens of teams to monitor. Teams can be siloed and disconnected from overall company goals, fracturing the organization and impairing alignment.
